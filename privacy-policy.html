Privacy Policy

For Allrounder99

Effective date: 27 June 2026

Allrounder99 is a cricket live score and match information app. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user information.

Information We Collect

Allrounder99 does not ask users to create an account or log in. The app does not collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, password, payment details, or address from users.

How the App Works

The app may use an internet connection to display cricket match information, fixtures, scorecards, commentary, history, and points table content. The app is designed for viewing cricket information only.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal user data with third parties because the app does not collect personal user data.

Cookies and Tracking

The app does not use cookies for user tracking. The app does not use advertising tracking or personal profiling.

Children's Privacy

Allrounder99 does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that any personal information has been provided through the app, please contact us so it can be reviewed and removed.

Security

Since the app does not collect personal user data, no personal user database is maintained by the app. We still aim to keep the app safe and stable for users.

Third-Party Content

The app may show cricket-related information from data sources or APIs. Third-party services may have their own privacy practices. Users should review those policies where applicable.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact Us

For privacy questions or support, contact the app developer using the developer contact email provided on the Google Play listing.

This Privacy Policy applies only to the Allrounder99 mobile app.
